About Cody Smith PT:

This site has been developed to provide online personal training plans and fitness guidance in the most simplistic, straight-forward manner possible. We want to give you the most direct route to your fitness goals and living a healthy, pain-free lifestyle. That doesn't mean the journey will be easy, but at least finding the right answers doesn't have to be so hard.

Cody Smith is a NASM certified personal trainer with years of experience and countless hours helping clients improve their lives through fitness. He also holds various cerfications in corrective exercise, weight loss, and nutrition. His other interests include hiking, obstacle course racing, mixed martial arts, web design, and traveling.
